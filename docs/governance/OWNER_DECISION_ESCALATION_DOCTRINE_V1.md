## Core rule

When canonical-boundary uncertainty appears in sovereign zones, it must be escalated to owner decision.
It must not be silently bypassed.

## Mandatory trigger zones

1. sovereignty boundaries,
2. external exposure decisions,
3. deletion of canonical surfaces,
4. authority boundary changes,
5. storage role changes,
6. restore guarantee changes,
7. visibility restriction changes,
8. irreversible doctrine mutations.

## Escalation protocol

1. mirror the exact conflict,
2. name risk and impact,
3. map available bounded options,
4. request explicit owner decision,
5. record decision in registry.
