# DIRECTED_EVOLUTION_CROSS_CLUSTER_BOUNDARY_RULES_V1

Status:
- boundary_state: `ACTIVE`
- enforcement_mode: `MANDATORY`

## Purpose

Prevent authority leakage between clusters and block cross-cluster drift.

## Hard Boundary Rules

### B1 — Visual cluster cannot dictate truth cluster

1. C6 (`visual_language_theme_interface_maturity`) may improve presentation only.
2. C6 cannot alter C1 truth status semantics, evidence requirements, or verification verdict logic.

### B2 — Interactive/campaign modes cannot out-claim truth

1. C7 cannot present stronger certainty than C1 evidence allows.
2. No gamified/interactive surface may mask `PARTIAL/BLOCKED/NOT YET IMPLEMENTED`.

### B3 — Character layer cannot replace governance

1. C5 character surfaces are guardrails, not authority overrides.
2. Character guidance cannot mutate governance law or bypass gates.

### B4 — Growth cannot outrun hardening baseline

1. C4 promotion is blocked if C2 foundation/hardening baseline is red.
2. No monetization scaling claims before hardening readiness gates are met.

### B5 — Observation UX cannot fake execution state

1. C3 must preserve SOURCE_EXACT / DERIVED / PLACEHOLDER boundaries.
2. No "live" presentation may claim completeness beyond actual live implementation.

### B6 — Cross-link allowed, authority transfer forbidden

1. Candidates may reference secondary clusters.
2. Secondary linkage does not transfer promotion authority.
3. Primary cluster rules remain decisive for admission and rollback.

## Violation Handling

1. Any boundary breach => candidate state `BLOCKED`.
2. Promotion claim invalid until boundary conflict is resolved.
3. Breach must be logged with evidence path in candidate ledger.
