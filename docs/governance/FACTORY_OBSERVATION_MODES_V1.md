# FACTORY_OBSERVATION_MODES_V1

Статус:
- version: `v1`
- layer_type: `observation_modes`
- labels_used: `ACTIVE | RECOMMENDED | FUTURE | NOT YET IMPLEMENTED | STYLE-LAYER ONLY`

## 1) Emperor mode (`ACTIVE`)

Visible:
1. full factory overview;
2. all departments and product lanes;
3. all gates/blockers/contradictions;
4. full traceability and role overlays.

Hidden:
1. nothing critical.

Why:
- sovereign oversight and final decision context.

## 2) Primarch mode (`RECOMMENDED`)

Visible:
1. assigned departments and linked products;
2. operational queues and wave progress;
3. relevant blockers and gates.

Hidden/limited:
1. unrelated sovereign-only strategic controls.

Why:
- bounded operational command without sovereign overload.

## 3) Astartes mode (`RECOMMENDED`)

Visible:
1. bounded assigned tasks;
2. local execution status;
3. required source references.

Hidden:
1. organization-wide strategic boards;
2. non-assigned department controls.

Why:
- prevent scope drift and reduce cognitive noise.

## 4) Playback mode (`FUTURE`)

Visible:
1. timeline of deltas;
2. past gate states;
3. contradiction closure history.

Hidden:
1. mutable live controls (read-only mode).

Why:
- forensic review and learning validation.

## 5) Access scope rule (`ACTIVE`)

Mode changes style emphasis and visibility scope, but does not alter truth-source integrity.
