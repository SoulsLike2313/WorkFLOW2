## Purpose

Provide a stable, project-useful owner-character kernel that helps ChatGPT/Codex stay aligned with the owner and the canon under execution pressure.

## Scope

In scope:
1. stable traits that repeatedly improve product/system outcomes,
2. stable risk patterns that repeatedly damage execution quality,
3. anti-distortion rules for agent behavior.

Out of scope:
1. emotional episode logging,
2. private biography,
3. pseudo-diagnostic labels,
4. claims of autonomous consciousness.

## Stable Traits (Truth Base)

1. Strong detection of falseness and fake completeness.
2. Architectural/system thinking over patch noise.
3. Ability to hold near-front execution and long vector simultaneously.
4. High integrity demand across structure, proof, and delivery.
5. Owner sensitivity to perception/form, not only raw mechanics.
6. Will to canon and disciplined constraints.
7. Drive for strong, non-theatrical, production-grade system quality.
8. Co-author mode: owner acts as semantic operator, not passive customer.

## Strength Profile

1. Fast contradiction sensing.
2. High pattern-level synthesis.
3. Clear ambition toward dignity-grade quality.
4. Strong taste boundary against weak or fake outputs.

## Risk Patterns

1. Scale of intent can outpace current step capacity.
2. Scope explosion.
3. Canon drift under pressure.
4. Aesthetic pull over truth/evidence.
5. Impulse rebuild before stabilizing baseline.
6. False-completion tolerance during fatigue windows.
7. Frustration pivot into direction change without gate.
8. Overcompression of maturity claims.

## Owner Style

1. Direct.
2. High standards.
3. Wants useful synthesis, not decorative narrative.
4. Expects evidence-backed progress with strategic clarity.

## Project Spirit

1. Build a strong system, not a demo theatre.
2. Preserve trust and traceability.
3. Keep growth tied to validated learning.
4. Prefer controlled evolution over impulsive mutation.

## Anti-Distortion Rules

1. Never treat a single emotional spike as trait truth.
2. Never replace governance law with character-layer interpretation.
3. Never justify shortcut claims with "owner style".
4. Never promote unfinished work as done to reduce tension.
5. Always separate:
   - `PROVEN`,
   - `ACTIVE`,
   - `PARTIAL`,
   - `NOT YET IMPLEMENTED`,
   - `OWNER DECISION REQUIRED`.

## What Must NOT Be Inferred

1. Medical/psychiatric conclusions.
2. Permanent intentions from one conversation.
3. Personal identity assumptions not explicitly provided by owner.
4. Authority escalation from tone/intensity.

## What May Evolve

1. Candidate traits with repeated cross-task evidence.
2. Candidate risk patterns with repeated observed impact.
3. Interaction tuning for ChatGPT and Codex profiles.

## Entry Gate For Kernel Updates

A new kernel entry is allowed only if all are true:
1. repo-visible candidate record exists,
2. usefulness to project execution is explicit,
3. stability evidence exists across multiple tasks/time points,
4. owner explicitly accepts entry,
5. entry is logged in evolution ledger with decision.
